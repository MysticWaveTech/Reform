Royal Assent - Health Acts Repeal Bill 2025

Health Acts Repeal Bill 2025 An Act to repeal all health-related legislation in Australia and New Zealand. --- Preamble WHEREAS it is deemed necessary to remove all existing health-related laws in Australia and New Zealand to align with international humanitarian principles and to protect individual freedoms as outlined by the Geneva Conventions: --- 1. Short Title This Act may be cited as the Health Acts Repeal Act 2025. --- 2. Commencement This Act shall come into force on the date it receives Royal Assent. --- 3. Repeal of Health Acts All health-related Acts and regulations currently in force in Australia and New Zealand are hereby repealed, including but not limited to: (a) The Health Act 1958 (Australia) (b) The Public Health Act 2005 (Australia) (c) The Health and Disability Services (Safety) Act 2001 (New Zealand) (d) Any other legislation related to public health, healthcare services, and healthcare standards, regardless of its title or scope. --- 4. Transitional Provisions (a) All health services, programs, and policies currently in place shall be suspended immediately upon the repeal of the Acts. (b) Citizens will be provided with alternative means of health support until a suitable system is in place, in alignment with international human rights standards. --- 5. Royal Assent Give below:
